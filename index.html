Why should we care about litter?

Although leaving one plastic water bottle on the beach may seem just the ordinary, this accumulation of trash will enter the ocean, killing marine animals that either consume or get harmed by the litter. This not only affects the ocean, but also the air as many chemicals are released as trash decomposes. It will continue to damage our cities' air and, consequently, our lungs. Litter can cause the spread of diseases to not only animals, but to humans as well.
