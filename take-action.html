Take Action

How can you contribute?

While the dangers of litter are being combatted through large amounts of local government spending, ordinary citizens such as us can help change the environment too through less money and more actions.

Recycling Centers

Begin by regularly going to recycling centers to take out any waste you have at home. This way, you are part of a communal process and will feel accomplished when you take a bit of time out of your day once a week to do so!

Stream Cleanups

Next, you should try attending a stream cleanup. These are typically held in the fall and spring season. Remember to wear long sleeve shirts and long pants when you're doing the cleanup! Similar to the previous step, being part of a communal process will make you feel more motivated and accomplished; you're not just helping yourself, but you're watching others help the environment too.

Bring a bag

Finally, whenever you go outside to have a picnic, make sure to always bring a plastic bag with you to put your garbage in. Always secure the bag so no trash falls out. This can prevent trash consumption from local animals; if you also find trash on the sidewalk, don't just leave it there! Pick it up and put it into your bag.
